Leopard

The African Leopard, also called the panther, is a carnivorous mammal of the Felidae family. It is the largest of the leopard subspecies.

Owl

The Great Horned Owl (Bubo virginianus), of the owl family, is one of the most common large birds of prey in Canada.

Deer

The red Deeris a large deer of the temperate forests of Europe, North Africa, Western Asia and America.
